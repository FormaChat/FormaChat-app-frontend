The Problem

Running a business means juggling countless responsibilities. But what happens when customers reach out with questions after hours? Or when you're in meetings and miss important inquiries?

Every missed conversation is a missed opportunity. Customers move on. Questions go unanswered. And you're left wondering what you could have done differently.

Traditional chatbots feel robotic and can't answer specific questions about your business. Hiring 24/7 support staff is expensive and often impractical for smaller businesses.

Meet Formachat

Formachat is your AI-powered customer support assistant that truly understands your business. You provide detailed information about your operations (kept completely confidential), and our AI uses that knowledge to have natural, helpful conversations with your customers.

Whether it's 2 PM or 2 AM, your customers get accurate answers instantly. Meanwhile, you get complete visibility into every interaction.
